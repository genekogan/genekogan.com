<p>
	Tip of the Tongue is a collaboration with dancer/choreographer <a href="http://www.courtneygiannone.com/">Courtney Giannone</a> on a performance piece combining dance, aerialists, cyr wheel spinning, and live projection.  
</p>
<p>
	The piece was commissioned by <a href="http://www.streb.org/">STREB Extreme Action</a> as part of their Emerging Artists program, and performed live at the STREB Action Mechanics Lab in Brooklyn, NY, on April 26 and 27, 2012. I designed projections for the back wall and the floor which I controlled live in correspondence to the dancers and performers.
</p>
