<p>
	Bezier ribbons is a very simple process which continuously draws a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/B%C3%A9zier_curve">bezier curve</a> to the screen, whose end points and control points are randomly moving around. The below program allows you to draw four paths for each of the four points of the bezier curve, by click-dragging your mouse on it.
</p>
