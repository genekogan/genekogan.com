<!description>Beat-slicing pop music tracks via EchoNest Remix</!description>

<p>
	Some fun with <a href="http://the.echonest.com">EchoNest</a>'s <a href="http://code.google.com/p/echo-nest-remix/">Remix API</a>.  I took a selection of pop songs and then stripped off every beat but the first one of each bar, i.e. 1234 1234 1234 becomes 1 1 1. The victims are "Amber" by 311, "Bad Romance" by Lady Gaga, and "Angel Echoes" by Four Tet.
</p>
