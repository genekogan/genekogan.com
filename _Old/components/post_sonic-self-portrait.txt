<p>
	An attempt at sonifying my face, inspired by <a href="http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~rbd/">Roger Dannenberg</a> at Carnegie Mellon. I converted a photo of my face into lines using a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hough_transform">Hough transform</a>, and then fed them to <a href="http://www.klingbeil.com/spear/">SPEAR</a> which interpreted them as sonic partials. It sounds extraterrestrial.
