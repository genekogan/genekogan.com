	In summer of 2010 I traveled with some friends in the Karakoram highway stretching from western China, Xinjiang province, to the northern part of Pakistan in Hunza Valley. During this trip, I compiled a set of field recordings I took along the way, which later became part of <a  href="http://www.alongthekarakoram.com">Along the Karakoram</a> -- More information on this project <a href="../works/along-the-karakoram.html">can be found here</a>.
