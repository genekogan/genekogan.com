Gene Kogan is an artist and programmer who is interested in generative systems, machine learning, and the application of emergent technology into artistic and expressive contexts. He writes code for live music, dance, theater, performance, and visual art. He contributes to numerous open-source software projects and frequently gives workshops and demonstrations on topics related to code and art.

He is a contributor to OpenFrameworks, Processing, and p5.js, an adjunct professor at Bennington College, and a former artist-in-residence at Eyebeam. [CV] [Contact]
