Tip of the Tongue is a collaboration with dancer and choreographer Courtney Giannone on a performance piece combining dance, aerialists, cyr wheel spinning, and live projection.

The piece was commissioned by STREB Extreme Action as part of their Emerging Artists program, and performed live at the STREB Action Mechanics Lab in Brooklyn, NY, on April 26 and 27, 2012. I designed projections for the back wall and the floor which I controlled live in correspondence to the dancers and performers.
