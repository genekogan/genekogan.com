In summer of 2010 I traveled with some friends in the Karakoram highway stretching from western China, Xinjiang province, to the northern part of Pakistan in Hunza Valley. During this trip, I compiled a set of field recordings I took along the way, which later became part of Along the Karakoram -- More information on this project can be found here.
