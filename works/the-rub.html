The Rub

A dark and cynical reimagining of Hamlet, directed by Nessa Norich, produced by The Tremor Collective, and performed over two weekends at Magic Futurebox in Sunset Park, Brooklyn, NY. An immersive space was created by projection-mapping the floor, back wall, and eight pillars along the sides of room. The audience was free to walk onto the stage, confusing the boundaries between performers and spectators.
