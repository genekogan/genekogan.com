Acoustic Portraits was a video piece made during my stay in Davao, Philippines as part of The UnifiedField nomadic artist-in-residency, and exhibited at Fete Dela WSK in Manila in November 2013. A recording of four local musicians from Davao City playing a mix of traditional and world instruments was slowed to 1/10th of the original speed to create a non-temporal portrait of their music.

The objective was to capture the sound of the instrument at a precise moment and hold it, as though looking at a photograph. With time stretching software, we can nearly freeze the sound. This dilation of time suggests qualities in the timbre of the instruments that are otherwise too transient to hear at normal playback, bringing out their richness in a new way.
