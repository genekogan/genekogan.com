Tweets by @RobotShakespear


Robot Shakespeare is a cybernetic reincarnation of William Shakespeare, trapped inside of a Twitter account which haphazardly generates some stream-of-consciousness prose every 15 minutes, forever.

It is driven by a script which downloads the bard's complete works, models his word-to-word transition probabilities, and does a random walk. How long before Robot Shakespeare recreates the complete works of Christopher Marlowe?


I might call him
A thing divine, for nothing natural
I ever saw so noble.
