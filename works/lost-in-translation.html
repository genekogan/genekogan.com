Some experiments in language translation loops, using the Google Translate API. The first process copied our revered Bill of Rights, translated it into French, then the result into Chinese, then that result into Spanish, then Afrikaans, Dutch, Czech, Arabic, Japanese, and then back into English. One translation is sure to produce some artifacts -- a chain of translations is certain to amplify them.
